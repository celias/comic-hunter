Something went wrong or took too long. Let's capture it.

Ask me:

1. What happened?
2. Why did it happen? (Push past "I messed up" — what's the systemic reason?)
3. What's the rule going forward to prevent this?

Then append a new entry to LESSONS.md in this format:

### YYYY-MM-DD: [short title]

**What happened:** [one sentence]
**Why:** [root cause]
**Rule going forward:** [concrete, enforceable rule]

After adding the entry, check if this lesson should also become a rule in CLAUDE.md. If so, suggest the update.
